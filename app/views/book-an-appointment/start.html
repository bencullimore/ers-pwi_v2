Log In to e-Referral Service

Book, change or cancel your appointment online by entering your details on the next page, then click 'Log In'.

For security reasons, you have three attempts to enter your correct details. If you are unable to log in, please contact your GP practice.

You will need your reference number (shown at the top of your appointment letter) and your password (which your GP practice gave you). Please contact your GP practice if you do not have your letter or password.
